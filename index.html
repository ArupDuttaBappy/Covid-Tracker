Frequently Asked Questions
How do you get these data?
We are using Coronavirus COVID19 API to populate data appropriately. Covid19API is a free API for data on the Coronavirus.
Data is sourced from Johns Hopkins CSSE.
Is the subscription (daily/weekly) free?
Yes! Both daily and weekly subscription are free.
You can use multiple email address to subscribe.
I think my country is not on the list. What ca I do?
We are limited by the data Covid19API providing us.
Please redirect to this site to know current COVID-19 situation in your country.
A false information observed. How to report?
We are limited by the data Covid19API providing us.
Please redirect to this site to get all latest COVID information.
How to unsubscribe from subscription list?
Please follow the procedures of your mail server to unsubscribe from mailing list.
